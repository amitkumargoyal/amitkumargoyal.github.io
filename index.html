Who am I?

Economics Coach & Academic Specialist based in Delhi & Abu Dhabi

I run Econschool, a preparatory program for students aspiring to MA/PhD in Economics at institutions like DSE, ISI, and international programs. I am affiliated with NYU Abu Dhabi and have an MA in Economics from DSE and a BA from Delhi University. My academic background includes doctoral work at ISI Delhi. I care deeply about mathematical rigour in economics education and about building a global community of economics enthusiasts.

Research Interests

Microeconomic Theory

Consumer and producer theory, general equilibrium, welfare economics, and the mathematical foundations of economic reasoning. Interested in both classical results and modern extensions.

Game Theory

Strategic interaction, equilibrium concepts, mechanism design, information economics, and auction theory. Particular interest in the interplay between theory and real-world institutions.

Economic Education

Designing rigorous, mathematically demanding curricula for graduate-level preparation. Translating landmark research — Akerlof, Milgrom, Diamond-Dybvig — into problems solvable at MA level.
